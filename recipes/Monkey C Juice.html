Monkey C Juice Recipe

Description

A great Vitamin C and potassium boost, and only 2 ingredients! This is a copycat of one of my favorite Robeks juices that has unfortunately been retired. Make sure to use freshly-squeezed juice, otherwise the flavor just isn't the same with store-bought.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces freshly-squeezed orange juice
  • 1 medium banana, peeled and quartered

Steps

  1. Place juice and banana in blender. Blend on high until smooth and frothy. about 30 seconds. Serve immediately.
